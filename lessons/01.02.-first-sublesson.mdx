## Shifting Mindset - Wealth Guiding Principles
**CURIOUS, NOT FEARFUL**
- If we want to go farther and do what matters most, we must stay curious.  Be Curious before being fearful

**COURAGE OVER COMFORT**
- This is the most important of these truths. Nothing of significance has ever been attempted or accomplished surrounded in comfort. Take Action
